Experiences

Water slides

At Honeydew we have a variety of colorful waterslides for all ages. Ride the slides alone, with a friend, or using a swim ring. The choice is yours!

Pools

At our parks you can find pools of different varying depths, between 0.5 m and 2 m. Check out our wave pool, Junior pool or the Olympic pool. Something for everyone! 

Classes

For those looking to enjoy a little fitness, schedule your spot in any of our classes. We offer Aqua Zumba, surfing in the wave pool and floatie aerobics. 

Saunas

There are so many ways to relax at Honeydew: the steam sauna, dry sauna, Finnish sauna and smoke sauna.
